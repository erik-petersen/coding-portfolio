I came to find that coding is actually a lot like baking. Both occupations manipulate ingredients using tools. I love manipulating ingredients and I love manipulating code. I'm amazed and excited about how many frameworks and technologies there are to utilize. Since graduating from The Iron Yard, I've enjoyed spending time learning some of these new tools. At the moment, I'm exploring Bootstrap and diving deeper into Angular.
