Homeshare

Homeshare was our final project at The Iron Yard. The mobile app was created to allow roommates or family members stay organized with daily household chores and have fun while doing it! Our group cross-collaborated by using an Angular front-end that spoke to a Rails back-end.

Etsy Clone

Yes, we cloned Etsy. What tech education would be complete without the shady cloning of a popular website? Our entire class came together. My partner and I took on the categories page using an Angular front-end talking to Etsy's API in order to bring in everything that populates the page. We made use of localstorage when a user clicks on an item.

The Catalan Opening

The Catalan Opening is a popular chess opening that was created in the 1920s. In this app, I used jQuery to navigate forward and backward through the first nine moves. There is also an optional play/pause feature.
